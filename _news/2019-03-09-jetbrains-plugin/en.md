Programmers developing in one of the JetBrains IDEs ([IntelliJ IDEA](https://www.jetbrains.com/idea/), [PyCharm](https://www.jetbrains.com/pycharm/), [WebStorm](https://www.jetbrains.com/webstorm/specials/webstorm/webstorm.html), â€¦) can submit their solutions to Dodona using a [plugin](https://plugins.jetbrains.com/plugin/11166-dodona) developed by Pieter De Clercq en Tobiah Lissens. Their [GitHub](https://github.com/thepieterdc/dodona-plugin-jetbrains) repository contains instructions for installing and using the plugin, and accepts issues and pull requests. All bug reports, feature requests or new ideas are welcomed.
